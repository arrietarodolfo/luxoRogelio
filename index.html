At Luxo Mobile Car Detailing

We bring the high-end automotive detailing experience directly to your home or workplace. We are a company passionate about car care, specializing in offering professional at-home detailing services, designed to restore and protect the original beauty of each vehicle.

Backed by certifications from industry-leading brands like Rupes, Gtechniq, and Meguiar's, we guarantee professional-level results, with cutting-edge products and techniques. Whether you're looking for a deep interior cleaning, paint polishing, ceramic protection, or complete maintenance, at Luxo we combine precision, quality, and convenience in every service

Our commitment is to provide personalized attention, impeccable finishes, and a luxurious experience without you having to leave home. At Luxo, the shine is just the beginning.

Luxo Premium

Elevate your car's shine with our Luxo Premium package. This comprehensive detailing service includes advanced interior care and a machine-applied Carnauba wax for superior gloss and protection, keeping your vehicle looking its best for months.

SUV Price: $180
